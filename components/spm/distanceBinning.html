Distance Binning

Component Index > SPM > Utilities > DB

Allows faster processing of dynamics that reference the entire field of particles (Cohesion, Alignment, Separation) by processing only points in a local area (Bin) at a time. Distance binning is a form of space partitioning and is unnecessary for most dynamics (as they don't care about their neighbour particles in the field).

Inputs

NameIDDescriptionType
DList of dynamics to apply distance binning to. These should only be dynamics that reference their neighbouring particles (Such as Cohesion, Separation, Alignment...)Generic Data

Outputs

NameIDDescriptionType
DParameters used to modify the vector field of the Vector Field Creater that this must be wired intoGeneric Data
