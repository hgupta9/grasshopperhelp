Number Interpolation

Component Index > SPM > Utilities > Num Interp

Interpolates N-dimensional points based on surrounding M-dimensional vectors

Inputs

NameIDDescriptionType
SPTree of lists of doubles representing N-dimensional points. These must be parallel to the list of incoming Sample Vectors.Number
SVTree of lists of doubles representing M-dimensional vectors. These vectors must be parallel to the list of incoming Sample Points.Number
TPTree of lists of doubles representing N-dimensional points, these points will be the reference points for each interpolationNumber
RRadius from each test point to use in the interpolationNumber
p1 for linear, n for higher strengthsNumber

Outputs

NameIDDescriptionType
VTree of lists representing N-dimensional vectorsNumber
