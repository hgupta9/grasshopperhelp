StartDateThe date you wish to search from: yyyy-mm-ddText
EndDateThe date you wish to search to: yyyy-mm-ddText
LatitudeGeo-Latitude you wish to searchText
LongitudeGeo-Longitude you wish to searchText

hasGeoMust the photo contain Geo-Data?Boolean

PageItterate through pages by specifying them hereInteger
