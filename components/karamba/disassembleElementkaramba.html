Disassemble Element (karamba)

Component Index > Karamba > Karamba Model > Disassemble Element (karamba)

Decomposes beams and shells into their components.

Inputs

NameIDDescriptionType
ModelModel to which the elements to be disassembled belongModel
ElemBeam or shell to disassemble.Element

Outputs

NameIDDescriptionType
ElemElementElement
ElemIdIdentifier of the elementText
AxisLongitudinal axis of beam.Curve
CroSecCross section of the elementCrossSection
JointsJoints at the endpoints of the beam. '-1' means fixed, '0' means free, all other positive values represent the corresponding spring-stiffness. The first six values symbolize the DOFs of the joint at the starting point of the beam, the last six those at the end-point.Integer
MatMaterial of the elementMaterial
LCoSysPlane oriented according to the beams local coordinate system.Plane
ActiveTrue if the element is active.Boolean
BendingTrue if a beam has bending stiffness. Otherwise it is a truss.Boolean
ecce-locPart of beam eccentricity [cm] which is defined in local beam coordinates.Vector
ecce-gloPart of beam eccentricity [cm] which is defined in global coordinates.Vector
BucklingIf true cross section optimization of the element is done considering buckling. Deactivating buckling can be useful for simulating slender elements which you want to pretension in reality but not in the numerical model.Boolean
BklLenYBuckling length [m] of beam in local Y-direction.Number
BklLenZBuckling length [m] of beam in local Z-direction.Number
BklLenLTBuckling length [m] of beam for lateral torsional buckling.Number
lgDistance of transverse load [cm] from the center of shear of the cross section. Is positive if the load points towards the shear center and acts destabilizing for lateral torsional buckling.Number
NIINormal force [kN] that induces second order effects. Tension is positive, compression negative.Number
X-oriSet X-Orientation of the beam: Flips X-axis of the beam if it points in opposite direction to this vector.Vector
Z-oriNew orientation of the local Z-axis of beam.Vector
AlphaRotation angle about local X-axis [deg] in addition to orientation given by orientation vector.Number
