Utilization of Elements (karamba)

Component Index > Karamba > Karamba Results > Utilization of Elements (karamba)

Returns the utilization of beams or shells for each load case according to Eurocode 3. For shells the ratio between Van Mises Stress and yield stress is returned and buckling is not considered. 1 means 100%. By default all elements are included.

Inputs

NameIDDescriptionType
ModelModel with calculated displacementsModel
ElemIdsList of identifiers of elements that take part in cross section design. By default the whole structure is included.Generic Data
LCaseNumber of loadcase for which to retrieve results. By default results for all load cases are retrieved.Integer
nSamplesNumber of sample points where cross section forces and resistance are compared for cross section design.Integer
Elasttrue: elastic cross section design utilization; false: plastic cross section design utilization for beams. Default: true.Boolean
gammaM0Material safety factor in case that buckling does not lead to failure.Number
gammaM1Material safety factor in case that buckling leads to failure.Number
Details?If true details regarding intermediate results are output at 'Details'.Boolean

Outputs

NameIDDescriptionType
ModelModel with calculated displacementsModel
UtilUtilization [] of all elements.Number
Util-NContribution of axial force to the total utilization.Number
Util-VyRatio between governing and ultimate shear force in Y-direction.Number
Util-VzRatio between governing and ultimate shear force in Z-direction.Number
Util-MtRatio between governing and ultimate torsional moment.Number
Util-MyContribution of bending moment about to local Y-axis to the total utilization.Number
Util-MzContribution of bending moment about to local Z-axis to the total utilization.Number
DetailsIntermediate values of the design calculations.Text
